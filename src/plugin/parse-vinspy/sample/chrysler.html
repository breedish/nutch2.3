Vehicle Comments

  • Comments: Sensibility and practicality define the 2014 Chrysler Town & Country! If you're looking to transport your family comfortably, this could be the minivan for you! It comes equipped with exciting features such as DVD player, stow 'n go, roof rack, and much more! With only one previous owner, you don't want to miss this opportunity!

    An American Icon. This 7 passenger van has not yet reached the 20,000 mile mark! All of the premium features expected of a Chrysler are offered, including: telescoping steering wheel, a power rear cargo door, and leather upholstery. Under the hood you'll find a 6 cylinder engine with more than 270 horsepower, and for added security, dynamic Stability Control supplements the drivetrain.

    We pride ourselves on providing excellent customer service. Looking for reliability and luxury in your minivan? Look no further. The Chrysler Town & Country has everything you need! Stop by our dealership or give us a call for more information.
